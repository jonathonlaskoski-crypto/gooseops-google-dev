**Success Indicators**:
- 40% reduction in emergency repairs through predictive maintenance

**Experience Qualities**: Intelligent, Predictive, Seamless

**Complexity Level**: Complex Application (advanced functionality with ML/AI capabilities)

**Primary User Activity**: Interacting and Creating (technicians actively engage with intelligent systems)

## Essential Features

### Core Operational Features
1. **Smart Job Management**
   - Offline job prioritization with ML scoring
   - Real-time route optimization with multi-constraint solving
   - Dynamic resource allocation and load balancing
   - Intelligent demand forecasting and capacity planning

2. **AI-Powered Field Assistance**
   - Copilot vision for equipment identification and troubleshooting
   - Contextual chat assistance for technical problems
   - Predictive job quality assessment
   - Automated compliance monitoring

3. **Advanced Camera & Documentation**
   - High-quality photo capture with compression
   - Computer vision analysis of equipment conditions
   - Automated task documentation with AI suggestions
   - Quality assurance through image analysis

4. **Predictive Analytics Dashboard**
   - Equipment failure prediction models
   - Customer satisfaction forecasting
   - Inventory demand optimization
   - Performance trend analysis

### ML/AI Intelligence Features

1. **Predictive Equipment Maintenance**
   - IoT sensor data analysis for failure prediction
   - Maintenance scheduling optimization
   - Cost impact assessment for equipment issues
   - Automated maintenance recommendations

2. **Smart Inventory Management**
   - Parts demand forecasting based on job predictions
   - Supplier lead time optimization
   - Safety stock calculations with ML models
   - Automated reorder point calculations

3. **Dynamic Pricing & Revenue Optimization**
   - Market condition-based pricing adjustments
   - Customer tier and urgency multipliers
   - Competitive analysis integration
   - Profit margin optimization

4. **Automated Quality Assurance**
   - Computer vision for work quality assessment
   - Compliance monitoring with regulatory knowledge
   - Real-time quality score predictions
   - Training recommendations based on performance

5. **Contextual Learning System**
   - Continuous learning from technician actions
   - Best practice identification and sharing
   - Knowledge base auto-updates
   - Federated learning across technician network
