## What is GooseOps?

GooseOps Neural Empire is a comprehensive operations platform built specifically for beverage installation and HVAC-R field service companies. It serves two primary audiences:

### **GooseOps Unlocked** - Executive Command Center
Full-featured platform for directors, executives, and office staff with advanced AI orchestration, business intelligence, and revenue generation tools.

### **GooseOps Light** - Field Technician Mobile App
Streamlined mobile interface for field technicians with offline capabilities, AI assistance, and essential job management tools.

**Built for RDS:** Retail Dispense Solutions serves major convenience store chains (QuikTrip, 7-11, Pilot, BP, RaceTrac, Circle K) and multi-site retail operations with expertise in beverage equipment installation (Coca-Cola Freestyle, FBD dispensers, Micromatic systems) and commercial HVAC-R services (walk-in coolers, ice machines, climate control systems).
